Customize your trip

TAILOR YOUR TRIP TO YOUR INTERESTS

All of Mountain Kick’s tour packages and the travel itineraries have been designed by veteran mountaineers and expert guides to bring a complete experience of the Himalayas in the limited number of days that are available.

But, you can always design your own trip. You can customize your holiday to choose the places you want to visit, the pace of your travel, the dates for your journey and the group with which you will travel.

Details, details, details.

Personalize to the minutest detail of your travel. Choose the sites you want to see, the routes you want to take, the stops you want to make and the places you want to stay and dine. Let us prepare an itinerary based on your requirements or you can prepare an itinerary yourself.

Your own experience.

The Himalayas have a variety of experiences to offer so you can pick and choose or just choose them all. You may like high-altitude trekking, or mountain biking, or visiting cultural spaces, or mixing with the locals, or maybe seeing the wildlife. We will take you wherever you want.

With your family and friends.

Choose the people you travel with and the size of your group. Come with your family, friends, or colleagues and plan group activities according to your interests.

Talk to us.

Tell us your preferences and the budget that we have to work with. Mountain Kick experts will design a custom itinerary for review and feedback. Add a few places or cut some activities till we arrive at a package that suits you the best. We are always ready to accommodate all your desires because it is your holiday.
